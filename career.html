Neuroscientist

When I grow up, I want to be a neuroscientist. A neuroscientist is a medical scientist who works to understand the nervous system. They design and carry out experiments to understand more about the brain and nervous system. They create better understandings of down syndrome, autistic spectrum disorders, schizophrenia, and many others. Without neuroscientists, these topics would not be well explained.
